Fries

Description

The greasy potato sticks that a lot of people crave for.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of sliced russet potatoes (alternatively, use frozen fries)
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary or paprika
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. If you're not using frozen fries, slice potatoes into sticks and place into a bowl of cold water.
  2. Boil oil to around 350Â° F. If you're not sure and don't have a thermometer, place a fry and see if it immediately boils.
  3. Stir all other ingredients except the oil and fries into a bowl to make a batter. If it is too thin, add more flour; if too thick, add more water.
  4. Dip fries into batter and pat-dry with a towel.
  5. Boil fries into oil for approximately 4 minutes. Take out fries, pat-dry, and fry again for another 4 minutes. Serve and enjoy the crunchiness!
