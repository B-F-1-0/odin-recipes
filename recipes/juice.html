Orange Juice

Description

Freshly squeezed, this juice can be a healthy dose of vitamin C in the morning! But I actually also like to drink it for lunch or dinner. Not everything's for breakfast, y'know.

Ingredients

  • 4+ oranges
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Slice oranges in half.
  2. Using a squeezer, squeeze juice into a glass.
  3. Add as much sugar you like for taste.
